## Applied Instruction Update: Root Research-Agent Workflow

### Date

2026-05-24

### Trigger

The project needed a clearer collaboration structure for Codex and the user.

### Rule

For non-trivial work, use the loop in `AGENTS.md`: restate the task, identify
the research objective, resolve only decision-critical ambiguity, choose the
simplest useful approach, execute, evaluate, and record stable outcomes.

Yes.



Research Direction Summary: BO-Guided Synthetic Simulation for Posterior Foundation Models
Core Vision

Develop a system that learns probabilistic inference over physical systems using synthetic simulations.

The long-term idea is:

generate synthetic data from simulators,
use Bayesian Optimization (BO) to adaptively improve what simulations are generated,
train a Simulation-Based Inference (SBI) network,
eventually scale this into a foundation-model-style inference system for physical environments.

Target domain:

robotics,
physical systems,
sim-to-real settings,
latent physics inference.

real-world data is expensive.
